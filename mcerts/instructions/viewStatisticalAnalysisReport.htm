How to view a statistical analysis report :

There are three ways to view a statistical analysis report :

1. Through MCERTS summary :

From the initial screen, click [view] in the “MCERTS Summary” column for the desired validation report.

Click on the precision or bias value (highlighted in blue, red or green) for the analyte that you wish to see the statistical analysis report for.

2. Through Pass\Fail summary :

From the initial screen, click [view] in the “Pass\Fail Summary” column for the desired validation.

Click on the precision or bias result (values are PASS, FAIL, and SPASS) for the analyte that you wish to see the statistical analysis report for.

3. By looking at the reference materials in a validation and then looking at the compounds in the reference material :

From the initial screen click [view] under the “Reference Materials” column for the desired validation report.

Click [view] under the “Analytes” column for the desired reference material.

Click [view] under the “Statistical Analysis Report” for the desired analyte.

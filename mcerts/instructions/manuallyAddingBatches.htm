How to manually add batches to the MCERTS system

You can add all 11 batches at once or you can as you get them. There is no limit to how many batches you can add for each analyte, but you can only add a maximum of 11 all at once.

Step 1:

Click [manually add 11x2 matrix] on the first MCERTS screen :

Step 2 :

Enter the Equipment ID of the machine into the text box next to "Equipment ID". This is a number. e.g 356 or 158

The Standard Deviation (precision) % and Target Bias % must be as in the MCERTS documentation.

Step 3:

Enter concentrations for all your batches. For each batch the date and time must be unique. e.g. Dates 3/4/2005 00:00 and 3/4/2005 00:00 for replicate 1 and replicate 2 respectively would be incorrect because they are identical. Dates 3/4/2005 14:00 and 3/4/2005 15:00 are acceptable values.

Step 4:

Click the 'Add Matrix' button when you have entered your batches.
